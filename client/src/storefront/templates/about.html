About Cheezka

Our Story · Our Passion · Our Food

Welcome to Cheezka – Street Food Done Right

Cheezka is your go-to destination for the best street food in town. Located at Atif Road Ada Shreen Mor, Near Meezan Bank, we serve freshly prepared Pizzas, Burgers, Shawarmas, Pasta, and so much more — all at prices that make every bite worth it.

From our signature Cheezka Special Pizza loaded with premium toppings to our crispy Zinger Burgers and saucy Shawarmas, every item is crafted with love and fresh ingredients. We believe great food doesn't have to cost a fortune.

Whether you're dining in or getting it delivered, we guarantee a meal that satisfies. Call us on 0303-2793109 or 0303-2444109 to place your order today.
